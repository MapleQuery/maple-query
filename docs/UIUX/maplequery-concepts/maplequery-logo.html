Thick stroke, clean. The baseline.

Lightweight stroke. Elegant, premium feel.

Filled circle + handle. Bold favicon presence.

Dashed circle. Suggests scanning, in-progress, exploration.

8 — Double Ring

Two concentric circles. Depth, layers, nested queries.

9 — Click Search

Cursor inside lens. Interactive, click-to-query.

10 — Tilted

Rotated angle. Casual, indie, less corporate.

11 — Glint

Shine dot on the glass. Subtle polish detail.

Letter M in the lens. Monogram + search. Branded.
